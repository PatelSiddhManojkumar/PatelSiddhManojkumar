## ◢ About

I'm **Sid** — a B.Tech CSE (AI & Data Science) student at Parul University, Vadodara, graduating in **2027**. I build backend-first AI systems: RAG pipelines, LLM-served APIs, and the unglamorous plumbing that keeps them up. My projects run in front of **real users** — 100+ students on a bilingual mental-wellness RAG app, 40–45 athletes scoring matches daily — not in a notebook screenshot. I care about latency, retries, and uptime more than leaderboard demos.
